Scoring Calculators

The decathlon in comprised of ten individual events. Its goal is to measure overall athleticism of an athlete. Running, throwing, and jumping events are all included in the decathlon. To compare events across different skills, a scoring system was developed. Below are the calculators to convert your performance into the amount of points earned in the men's outdoor decathlon and indoor heptathlon.

How to Use

  • Field event marks should be entered as metric marks only using the format 0.00 (in meters)
  • Times should be entered using the format 0:00.00 (m:ss.dd)
  • All times should be entered as automatic timed.
    • If the event was hand-timed, add 0.14 seconds for 400m or 0.24 seconds for events shorter than 400m.
